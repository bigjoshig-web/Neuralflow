FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I need to know how to code?+
Not at all. NeuralFlow is built for non-technical users. Everything is configured using plain language and visual tools. If you can write an email, you can build an AI agent.
How long does it take to set up my first agent?+
Most users have their first agent live in under 10 minutes. Simply connect your data source, configure the agent behavior in plain English, and deploy. Our onboarding wizard guides you every step of the way.
Can I use NeuralFlow for my clients under my own brand?+
Yes — white-labeling is fully supported on Agency and Enterprise plans. Use your own domain, logo, colors, and brand name. Your clients will never see the NeuralFlow name anywhere.
What languages do the AI agents support?+
NeuralFlow agents support over 50 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Arabic, Portuguese, and many more. Language detection is automatic — the agent responds in whatever language the customer writes in.
Is my data safe and private?+
Absolutely. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We are SOC 2 Type II certified and fully GDPR compliant. Your training data is never used to improve our models and is never shared with third parties.
Can I cancel at any time?+
Yes, cancel anytime with no penalties. On monthly plans, you keep access until the end of your billing period. On annual plans, we offer a pro-rated refund within the first 30 days.
