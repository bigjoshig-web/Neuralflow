FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I need to know how to code?+
Not at all. NeuralFlow is built for non-technical users. Everything is configured using plain language. If you can write an email, you can build an AI agent.
How long does setup take?+
Most users have their first agent live in under 10 minutes. Connect your data, configure in plain English, and deploy. Our onboarding wizard guides you every step of the way.
Can I white-label this for my clients?+
Yes — full white-labeling is available on Agency and Enterprise plans. Use your own domain, logo, and colors. Your clients will never see the NeuralFlow name anywhere.
What languages do agents support?+
Over 50 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Arabic, and Portuguese. Language detection is automatic — the agent replies in whatever language the customer uses.
Is my data safe?+
Absolutely. End-to-end encryption, SOC 2 Type II certified, and fully GDPR compliant. Your training data is never used to improve our models and never shared with third parties.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. Cancel anytime, no penalties. Monthly plans stay active until the end of the billing period. Annual plans include a pro-rated refund within the first 30 days.
